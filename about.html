About CrowdSourced Cooking

Our Mission

Recipes should bring people together, not overwhelm them.

We believe cooking is about community. Every family has their own recipes, their own twists, their own secrets. Those recipes deserve to be shared, refined, and passed on.

CrowdSourced Cooking is a platform where cooks of all levels share recipes, vote on what works, and improve each other's creations. No gatekeeping. No ads. Just real food from real cooks.

How It Works

Share: Submit your recipes, from family favorites to experimental dishes.

Vote: Upvote the recipes you love.

Improve: Found a recipe you like? Make it your own with your own tweaks. That's MiMO.

Our Values

🤝 Community First: Recipes are meant to be shared, not hoarded.

📝 No Gatekeeping: All skill levels welcome. Your recipe counts.

🔀 Evolution: Recipes improve over time. MiMO keeps the original, improves the new.
