About CrowdSourced Cooking

Recipes should bring people together, not overwhelm them.

We built CrowdSourced Cooking because we got tired of the endless scroll— Pinterest giving us 10,000 "best chocolate chip cookies" from a thousand different blogs, each with a 20-paragraph life story before the recipe. You save dozens, maybe hundreds. And let's be honest: you never make them.

So we asked a simple question: What if recipes came from people who actually cook?

Our Philosophy

  • Real recipes, real cooks – No influencers, no SEO-bait. Just people sharing what they actually make.
  • Community-driven – Every recipe here is submitted by someone like you. Vote on the ones that work, MiMO the ones you want to make your own.
  • Simple, consistent, usable – No life stories. No wall of ads. Just ingredients, steps, and results.

What We're Building

A community of cooks, chefs, hobbyists, and professionals who love what they do. Share your kitchen wins. Improve on someone else's. Build a recipe collection that actually gets used.

Your kitchen. Your recipes. Your community.
