Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account and use CrowdSourced Cooking, we collect:

  • Account Information: Email address and display name when you register
  • Recipe Content: Recipes you submit, including ingredients, instructions, photos, and tags
  • MiMO Data: When you modify someone else's recipe, we link your version to the original

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide our recipe sharing platform services
  • Display your recipes to you and manage your account
  • Allow other users to view and interact with your public recipes
  • Keep MiMO'd recipes private to only you until you choose to make them public

3. Recipe Visibility

When you create or MiMO a recipe:

  • Original recipes you create are public and appear in search results
  • MiMO'd recipes are private by default - only you can see them
  • You can choose to "Make Public" any MiMO'd recipe you've created

4. Data Storage

Your recipes and account data are stored on Google Firebase (Firestore). We retain your data as long as your account is active.

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Access your account and recipes at any time
  • Edit or delete your recipes
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@crowdsourcedcooking.com
